Inner Dust (2022)

*3D interactive websiteA-frame + Dialogflow + MongoDB*

--- One's loneliness erupts, one turns into a speck of dust ---

Inner Dust present loneliness in a more complex way compared to the negative stereotype. 4 dust characters(chatbot), each with a different background story. Through designed dialogue, users find resonance with the robots and the multi-faces of loneliness.

The inputs and the choices users make are then visualized into a one-of-a-kind dust, act as ones loneliness. Every dust is displayed in a co-creation space, implying we are all in this together.

*group project: with Dodo Liu & Emerald Chen*

Lets Move the Trees (2021)

*assistive activity for Dementiaa proposal*

Let‚Äôs Move the Trees was a proposal for an ongoing project by Taipei Veterans General Hospital Center for Geriatrics and Gerontology.

The proposal combined a Gaga-inspired activity* with technology, a circled forest is projected on the floor. The journey begins with each patient consuming a candy(or daily pill). Each person is a seed, they acquire water, sunshine or wind to go through and nourish their body. The instructor will give example movements but expect the elderly to use imagination as how the sprouts grow inside of them. Together they make the projected tree grows as big as possible. (Kinect)

It aims as a social connection for the patients, the link between the circled group, the tree and the person him/herself. The gestures and movements focus more on expressive art therapy rather than physical training.
